01 byte OFF - entry # offset
01 byte ICON - icon used for entry(1 + element type)
	01(0x01) Fire
	02(0x02) Wind
	03(0x03) Earth
	04(0x04) Lightning
	05(0x05) Blizzard
	06(0x06) Water
	07(0x07) Explosion
	08(0x08) Forest
	09(0x09) Light
	10(0x0A) Darkness
	11(0x0B) Sword
	12(0x0C) Staff
	13(0x0D) Crossbow
	14(0x0E) Dagger
	15(0x0F) Poleaxe
	16(0x10) Chakram
	17(0x11) Red Shoe
18 byte Name - name of spell/move
02 byte C - cost of spell/move
01 byte T1 - target effect
	01(0x01) Restore HP
	02(0x02) Restore MP
	03(0x03) Restore SP
	04(0x04) Ally(?) Buff/Debuff
	05(0x05) Attack/Move(?) Damage
	06(0x06) Magic(?) Damage
	07(0x07) Enemy(?) Buff/Debuff
	08(0x08) Status Change
	15(0x0F) Special(Spellbinding Eye, Gravity, etc.)
01 byte T2 - target type
	01(0x01) One Ally
	02(0x02) Area Allies
	03(0x03) All Allies
	04(0x04) One Enemy
	05(0x05) Area Enemies
	06(0x06) All Enemies
	07(0x07) Enemy Line
	08(0x08) Self
	09(0x09) Unknown
	10(0x0A) Area Around Self(Beast King Blast)
	11(0x0B) Unknown
	12(0x0C) Unknown
	13(0x0D) Area Around Self(Taint of Wind)
	14(0x0E) Unknown
	15(0x0F) Unknown
02 byte STR - Strength added to base stat of character
02 byte POW - Power of spell/move
02 byte Range - Range the spell/move hits, based on T1 and T2
02 byte Cast1 - Time it takes to cast spell/move, lower = faster
02 byte Cast5 - Time it takes to cast spell/move, lower = faster
02 byte Rec - recovery time
02 byte Ani - animation of move
01 byte ?
01 byte DF - DOWN_FLG(from decompilation, likely a knock down flag)
02 byte IP - IP damage from spell/move
02 byte IPC - IP cancel damage from spell/move
02 byte KB - knockback from spell/move
01 byte E - element of spell/move
	00(0x00) Fire
	01(0x01) Wind
	02(0x02) Earth
	03(0x03) Lightning
	04(0x04) Blizzard
01 byte ES - element strength of spell/move
01 byte S - status effect bitflag(Add together for multiple effects)
	001(0x01) Poison
	002(0x02) Sleep
	004(0x04) Paralysis
	008(0x08) Confusion
	016(0x10) Plague
	032(0x20) Magic Block
	064(0x40) Move Block
	128(0x80) Death
01 byte % - % chance of status effect
02 byte ATK - attack stat change
02 byte DEF - defense stat change
02 byte ACT - action stat change
02 byte MOV - movement stat change
02 byte SP - special effect
		Special_Effects.txt
